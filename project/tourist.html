The Botanical Garden is a true gem, featuring stunning French-inspired gardens, a iconic glass greenhouse, and a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxation and photography. Tanguá Park offers breathtaking views with its lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and a picturesque tunnel that leads to a tranquil lake, making it an ideal spot for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The train ride to Morretes is an unforgettable journey through the lush Atlantic Forest, offering panoramic views of mountains, rivers, and valleys, while also providing a taste of the region's history and culture. The Ópera de Arame (Wire Opera House) is an architectural marvel, with its transparent structure surrounded by natural beauty, hosting a variety of performances that blend art and nature seamlessly. The Oscar Niemeyer Museum, with its striking eye-shaped design, is a haven for art enthusiasts, showcasing contemporary art exhibitions and celebrating the genius of its namesake architect. Each of these attractions highlights Curitiba's commitment to preserving nature, culture, and innovation, making it a must-visit destination. Last but not least, on the contrary, the house of the Lord, His Holy Temple, needs no comment.
